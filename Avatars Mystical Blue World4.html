Christ works in mysterious and wondrous ways. In my ministry, I share a deeply personal story of my experience with psychosis, which I liken to the experience a Pandoran creature experiences with a member of the Na'vi during a bond.

Direhorses for example can be tamed to aid their riders in the hunt and in battle. To bond with (or, in human terms, to "break") a direhorse, a Na'vi must mount the animal and connect their neural queue to one of the animal's antennae (or neural whips). Once queue and antenna touch, the feathery tendrils automatically intertwine as if possessed of free will. This elegant dance between the two elements creates a captivating visual, suggesting an intricate connection that transcends mere physical interaction. The tendrils, underpinned by a seemingly innate understanding, weave together gracefully, forming complicated patterns that reflect a harmonious union.
