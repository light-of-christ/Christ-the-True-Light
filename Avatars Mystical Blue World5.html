During my experience, I dug 10-foot holes across my acre of land, searching for women and children. I understand how insane that sounds, and I encourage you to read my story. However, on one particular occasion—my very last hole—I was told by a formless voice that I had opened Pandora's box. Did I? No, but we are opening it with Ai, and that is what he needed me to understand. By telling me that I had opened Pandora's box, I would later be able to see it as a sign, pointing the way for me. And I see it, in this movie. Only God can line these things up, again proving his existence in a tangible way for the very first time.
