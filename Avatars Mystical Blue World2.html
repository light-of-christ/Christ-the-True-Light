The film Avatar tells the story of Jake Sully, a paraplegic former Marine who steps into his brother Tommy's place in the Avatar Program by chance, as his DNA is a perfect match to that of his twin brother, who has been murdered. The program allows humans to remotely drive genetically engineered Na'vi bodies with their very own conscious. A theme in my work: "consciousness," as this is where our souls are. Simply put, Jake Sully's consciousness enters the Avatar in order to drive it. The Avatar is the wheel that I consistently mention throughout my work. The door Jesus spoke about is found in our minds.
